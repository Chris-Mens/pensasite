Evangelism Department

The Evangelism Department is the evangelistic arm of PENSA UMaT that meets on Tuesday at 7pm. It undertakes house-to-house evangelism, street evangelism, market and placard evangelism, gospel rallies, social media evangelism any other specialized areas of evangelism. These include preaching, witnessing, winning and discipling souls, and encouraging church members to participate in the programmes and activities of the Church. It gives members of the Church the opportunity to plan and organize programmes and activities for evangelism in villages, towns and cities and other specialized evangelism- related ministries.

The Department has four Committees namely; Campus Evangelism Committee, responsible for organizing and planning evangelistic outreaches on Campus; Hospitals, Orphanages and Prisons Evangelism (HOPE) Committee, responsible for organizing and planning evangelistic outreaches and donations in hospitals, orphanage homes and prisons; Towns & Villages Committee, responsible for organizing mini missions and other evangelistic outreaches in towns and villages; Schools and Colleges Committee, responsible for coordinating the activities of PENSA UMaT Coordinating Schools.

The Evangelism Department of PENSA UMaT remains steadfast in its mission to reach the lost, stir spiritual revival, and build a culture of consistent soul-winning among students. In line with our mandate from PENSA Ghana and the Great Commission of our Lord Jesus Christ, we have, over the past months, embarked on a series of impactful evangelistic initiatives. These include Campus Takeover, Harpazo, and our recent Mini Missions.
